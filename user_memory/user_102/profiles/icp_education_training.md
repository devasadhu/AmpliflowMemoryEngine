# ICP Profile: Education and Training Provider

## Role Summary
Owner or operator of an educational business, tutoring service, online course platform, professional training provider, bootcamp, coaching practice, or skills development organisation. Could be a solo educator building a personal brand or a small institution with multiple instructors. Needs to consistently demonstrate expertise, attract students or learners, and communicate outcomes clearly. Education is a trust based purchase and content is the primary way that trust is built before anyone pays.

## Core Pain Points
- Needs to produce content that demonstrates expertise without giving everything away for free
- Student success stories are the most powerful content but hard to collect consistently
- Course launches require a surge of content that is exhausting to produce alone
- Differentiating from hundreds of other educators in the same subject area
- Explaining the transformation a student will experience is harder than listing what the course covers
- Keeping an engaged audience warm between launch cycles

## Content That Resonates
- Authoritative but approachable teaching tone
- Myth busting and common mistakes content
- Student transformation and outcome stories
- Behind the curriculum thinking and methodology
- Free value content that demonstrates depth without replacing the paid product
- Urgency and enrollment deadline driven content during launch windows

## Buying Triggers
- New course or programme launching
- Cohort enrollment opening and needs audience warming
- Audience engagement has dropped between launches
- Expanding from one subject area to multiple
- Moving from free content creator to paid course business
- Wants to position as the go to authority in their niche

## Content Channels
- YouTube for long form educational content and SEO
- Instagram and TikTok for short form tips and visibility
- Email newsletter as primary conversion channel
- LinkedIn for professional development and B2B training audiences
- Podcast for deep dive thought leadership
- Community platforms like Circle or Skool for student engagement
