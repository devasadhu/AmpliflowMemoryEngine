# ICP Profile: Local Service Business Owner

## Role Summary
Owner-operator of a local or regional service business. Could be in food and hospitality, personal care, trades, repairs, events, or any hands-on service industry. Typically runs a small team of 1-15 people. Personally responsible for all business functions including marketing despite having no formal training in it. Deep expertise in their craft, limited bandwidth for anything outside of it.

## Core Pain Points
- Content creation feels foreign and time consuming
- No consistent posting schedule, goes quiet for weeks then posts in bursts
- Struggles to translate what makes their business special into words
- Sees competitors with polished social media and feels behind
- Cannot afford a marketing agency or full time content person

## Content That Resonates
- Warm, personal and community rooted tone
- Stories about the people behind the business
- Local pride and neighbourhood connection
- Before and after transformations
- Real customer moments and testimonials
- Seasonal promotions and offers framed naturally not salesy

## Buying Triggers
- Just opened a new location or expanded services
- A competitor is clearly winning on social media
- Slow season approaching and needs to drive footfall
- Just received a flood of positive reviews and wants to capitalise
- Wants to build reputation before a big event or launch

## Content Channels
- Instagram and Facebook primary
- WhatsApp broadcast lists for regulars
- Google Business profile updates
- Occasional email newsletter to loyal customers
