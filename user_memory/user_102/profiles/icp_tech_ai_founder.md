# ICP Profile: Tech and AI Product Founder

## Role Summary
Founder or co-founder of an early to growth stage technology or AI product company. Could be building SaaS, developer tools, AI agents, fintech, edtech, or any software product. Technical background is common but not universal. Understands the product deeply but struggles to communicate its value to non-technical buyers. Needs to build credibility, attract users, and stand out in an increasingly noisy AI landscape.

## Core Pain Points
- Product is technically impressive but hard to explain simply
- Content either goes too deep and loses non-technical readers or too shallow and loses technical credibility
- No time to write consistently while also building the product
- Needs to build an audience before the product is fully ready
- Differentiating from hundreds of similar sounding AI tools is genuinely hard

## Content That Resonates
- Clear and confident thought leadership
- Founder perspective and behind the build storytelling
- Honest takes on the industry including what is overhyped
- Use case driven content showing real world value
- Technical depth for developer audiences balanced with business outcomes for buyer audiences
- Early customer wins and case study style content

## Buying Triggers
- Product launching or coming out of beta
- Raising a funding round and needs investor facing content
- Competitor just got press coverage and they need to respond
- Developer community engagement is low despite good product
- Trying to move upmarket from SMB to enterprise buyers

## Content Channels
- LinkedIn primary for B2B and investor audience
- Twitter or X for developer and tech community
- Technical blog for SEO and credibility
- Product Hunt and launch platforms
- Newsletter for early adopters and waitlist
