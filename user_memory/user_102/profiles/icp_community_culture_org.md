# ICP Profile: Community and Cultural Organisation Manager

## Role Summary
Manager or director of a community organisation, cultural institution, business association, museum, arts collective, faith organisation, or alumni network. Responsible for member engagement, event promotion, and maintaining the organisation's public presence. Usually working with a small team or volunteers. The organisation has real community value but struggles to communicate it consistently and compellingly online.

## Core Pain Points
- Member engagement drops between major events
- Communicating the value of membership in a way that attracts new members
- Event promotion feels repetitive and formulaic
- Multiple stakeholders with different opinions on tone and messaging
- Very limited marketing budget, relies almost entirely on organic reach
- Content needs to serve very different audiences, existing members, prospective members, funders, and the general public

## Content That Resonates
- Inclusive, warm and community first tone
- Member and contributor spotlights
- Event previews and recaps with real atmosphere
- Local and cultural pride
- Educational and informative content that adds genuine value
- Content that makes existing members feel seen and valued

## Buying Triggers
- Annual membership renewal cycle approaching
- Major event or exhibition coming up needing promotion
- Declining engagement metrics or membership numbers
- New leadership wanting to modernise the organisations voice
- Grant application requiring evidence of community reach and digital presence

## Content Channels
- Facebook primary for established community members
- Instagram for events and cultural moments
- Email newsletter for member communications
- Website for credibility and SEO
- LinkedIn for corporate sponsors and partnerships
