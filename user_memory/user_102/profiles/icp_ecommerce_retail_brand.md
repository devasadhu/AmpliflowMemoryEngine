# ICP Profile: E-Commerce and Retail Brand Owner

## Role Summary
Founder or operator of an online or hybrid retail brand. Could be in fashion, homewares, accessories, beauty, streetwear, artisan goods, or any product-led business. May run a Shopify or custom store. Has some marketing awareness but struggles to produce content at the volume and consistency modern e-commerce demands. Often a one or two person operation trying to compete with bigger brands.

## Core Pain Points
- Product launches need content across multiple channels simultaneously
- Writing product descriptions that actually convert is harder than it looks
- Keeping social media active between launch cycles is exhausting
- Brand voice is inconsistent because multiple people or moods are writing
- Paid ads are expensive and organic content is the only scalable alternative

## Content That Resonates
- Aspirational but accessible tone
- Lifestyle and identity driven storytelling
- Behind the scenes of product creation and sourcing
- UGC style content that feels authentic not corporate
- Seasonal and trend aware messaging
- Clear calls to action without feeling pushy

## Buying Triggers
- New collection or product line launching
- Slow sales period needing organic traffic boost
- Brand refresh or repositioning underway
- Expanding from local market to national or international
- Just started running ads and needs organic content to match

## Content Channels
- Instagram and TikTok primary
- Email marketing for repeat customers
- Pinterest for discovery
- Product page copy and SEO content
- Influencer briefs and collaboration content
