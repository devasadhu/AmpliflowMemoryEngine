 
# ICP Profile: Industrial and Manufacturing Business

## Role Summary
Marketing, communications, or operations lead at an industrial, manufacturing, engineering, logistics, or production business. Could be in food manufacturing, aerospace components, construction materials, printing, packaging, automotive parts, pharmaceuticals, or any physical production industry. Content needs are B2B focused, targeting procurement managers, supply chain directors, and technical buyers. The purchase cycle is long, trust is built over months, and content must demonstrate reliability, compliance, and technical capability above all else.

## Core Pain Points
- Industry has historically relied on trade shows and direct sales, digital content is new and unfamiliar territory
- Strict controls on what operational data, processes, and client relationships can be disclosed publicly
- Technical specifications need to be communicated accurately without oversimplifying
- Long sales cycles mean content must nurture leads over months not days
- Safety, compliance, and certification messaging must be precise and never exaggerated
- Internal stakeholders are conservative and cautious about external communications

## Content That Resonates
- Authoritative, precise and specification driven tone
- Case studies framed around operational outcomes, efficiency gains, cost reduction, uptime improvement
- Compliance and certification led credibility content
- Behind the process and quality control content that builds supplier trust
- Industry trend commentary that positions the business as a sector expert
- Content that speaks directly to procurement and supply chain pain points

## Disclosure and Compliance Constraints
- Client identities and contract details require explicit approval before any public reference
- Operational throughput and capacity data may be commercially sensitive
- Safety incident data must never be referenced in marketing content
- Regulatory certifications must be quoted accurately with correct body names and standards
- Export control and trade compliance restrictions may limit what markets can be referenced publicly
- Environmental and emissions claims require verified data backing

## Buying Triggers
- Tendering for new contracts and needs digital credibility to support the bid
- Entering a new market or sector requiring awareness building
- ISO or industry certification achieved and needs communicating
- New production line, facility, or capability launching
- Trade show or industry event approaching
- Existing clients asking for more digital presence as part of supplier evaluation

## Content Channels
- LinkedIn primary for B2B buyer and procurement audience
- Industry trade publications and guest editorial
- Website case studies and technical white papers
- Email to existing client and prospect database
- YouTube for facility tours and process demonstrations
- Trade association platforms and directories
