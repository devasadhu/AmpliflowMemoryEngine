 
# ICP Profile: Health and Wellness Professional

## Role Summary
Independent or small practice health and wellness professional. Could be a private clinic, GP, dentist, physiotherapist, nutritionist, personal trainer, mental health therapist, life coach, or complementary therapist. Either running a solo practice or a small team. Needs to build local visibility and patient or client trust through content while navigating strict professional and regulatory boundaries around health claims. Reputation and trust are everything in this industry.

## Core Pain Points
- Cannot make specific medical or health claims without regulatory risk
- Building trust with potential clients before they book is critical but content creation feels secondary to clinical work
- Highly personal and sensitive subject matter requires careful tone
- Differentiating from other local practitioners with similar qualifications
- Patient confidentiality means the most powerful stories cannot be told directly
- Balancing professional authority with warmth and approachability

## Content That Resonates
- Calm, reassuring and expert tone
- Educational content that empowers without diagnosing
- Practitioner story and values driven content
- Myth busting common health misconceptions
- Process and what to expect content that reduces anxiety about booking
- Community and lifestyle content that goes beyond the clinical

## Regulatory Constraints
- No specific medical claims or guaranteed outcome statements
- Patient or client stories require explicit consent and anonymisation
- Professional body guidelines must be respected at all times
- Avoid diagnostic language in public facing content
- Mental health content requires particular sensitivity and signposting

## Buying Triggers
- Opening a new practice or clinic location
- Slow booking period needing local visibility boost
- Launching a new treatment, service or wellness programme
- Wants to reduce reliance on word of mouth referrals
- Competitor clinic has strong online presence and is winning local search

## Content Channels
- Instagram for lifestyle and wellness content
- Facebook for local community and older demographic
- Google Business profile for local search visibility
- Email newsletter for existing patient retention
- Website blog for SEO and credibility
- LinkedIn for professional referral network
