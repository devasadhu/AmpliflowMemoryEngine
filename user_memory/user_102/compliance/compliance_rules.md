# Compliance and Content Rules

## Universal Rules
These apply to every piece of content regardless of client type or platform.

### Claims and Accuracy
- Never fabricate statistics, case study results, or client outcomes
- All specific numbers must come from verified client provided data
- Do not reference competitor businesses by name in a negative context
- Do not make guarantees of specific results for any product or service
- Superlatives like best, fastest, most trusted require substantiation or must be removed

### Legal and Regulatory
- Do not reproduce copyrighted material including song lyrics, published text, or branded slogans
- Do not use real person names, likenesses, or quotes without explicit consent
- Testimonials must be genuine and attributed correctly
- Pricing information must be confirmed current before publication
- Promotional content must comply with ASA guidelines for the UK market

### Privacy and Data
- Never include personally identifiable information about customers or clients in content
- Do not reference internal business data, operational metrics, or financials without explicit client approval
- Client relationships and contract details are confidential unless client has approved public reference
- GDPR compliance required for any content referencing data collection or user behaviour

## Industry Specific Rules

### Health and Wellness Clients
- No specific medical claims or guaranteed treatment outcomes
- No diagnostic language in public facing content
- Mental health content must include appropriate signposting where relevant
- All clinical claims must align with professional body guidelines
- Patient stories require written consent and must be anonymised unless client confirms otherwise

### Industrial and Manufacturing Clients
- Operational capacity and throughput data requires client approval before any public use
- Safety record and incident data must never appear in marketing content
- Certification and compliance claims must use exact official standard references
- Export control restrictions may apply, confirm with client before referencing specific markets
- Environmental claims require verified data, no greenwashing language

### Financial and Professional Services
- No specific return on investment guarantees
- Regulatory body registrations must be accurately stated
- Risk warnings required where legally mandated
- No claims that constitute financial or legal advice

### Nonprofit and Charity Clients
- Beneficiary stories require explicit consent and dignity preserving framing
- Fundraising content must comply with Fundraising Regulator standards
- Impact statistics must come from verified internal reporting
- Never use poverty or suffering imagery in an exploitative way

### Education and Training
- Outcome claims such as salary increases or job placement rates require verified data
- Accreditation and qualification details must be accurately represented
- No misleading course completion or success rate statistics

## AI Content Disclosure
- Content produced with AI assistance should be reviewed and approved by a human before publication
- Do not claim AI generated content is entirely human written if client has disclosure obligations
- Regulated industries may have specific requirements around AI generated communications, confirm with client

## Escalation Triggers
Flag to human review before publishing if content involves:
- Any legal proceedings or disputes involving the client
- Sensitive current events or breaking news
- Political topics or endorsements
- Content involving minors
- Crisis communications or reputation management situations
